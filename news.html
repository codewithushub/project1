code with us in their actons

The EmpowerTech program in Kakuma Refugee Camp is dedicated to empowering displaced individuals with valuable technology skills to enhance their self-reliance and employability in the digital age. Through a range of activities, including digital literacy workshops, coding and programming courses, entrepreneurship training, mentorship, and access to technology resources, the program equips participants with essential skills. Graduates have already begun to secure freelance work and tech-related employment, contributing to income generation and fostering a sense of community. The recent expansion of the program's Tech Hub and partnerships with local tech companies for internships signal its growth and recognition on a global scale, highlighting its positive impact on empowering refugees through technology.
