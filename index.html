Fostering Education and Skill Development

In Kakuma, empowering women through technology is essential for their economic independence as well as their access to healthcare and education. Technology makes it possible to start businesses, work from home, and provide financial services, which lowers reliance on aid and promotes self-sufficiency. Additionally, it expands access to telemedicine and e-learning, improving knowledge and medical outcomes. In Kakuma, technology essentially empowers women by encouraging their economic independence and wellbeing.
