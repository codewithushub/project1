get to know more about us

Empowering Women with Technology is a dynamic initiative operating within Kakuma Refugee Camp, with a steadfast mission to empower women and girls through coding education. Our workshops and mentorship programs are designed to bridge the digital gender gap, providing women with essential tech skills to thrive in today's world. Our vision extends beyond education; we aspire to see these empowered women emerge as leaders in technology, driving innovation and making a lasting impact within the camp and beyond. We invite everyone who shares our vision to join us in fostering inclusivity, breaking down barriers, and using technology as a catalyst for positive change. At Empowering Women with Technology, we believe that coding is not just a skill; it's a gateway to empowerment and a brighter future. By advocating for gender equality in the tech industry and equipping women with the tools they need, we aim to create a more equitable and prosperous world where every woman has the opportunity to thrive in the digital age. Join us in our mission to empower women with technology, unlocking their potential and transforming communities

MISSION

To focuses on addressing the gender gap in technology by creating an inclusive and equitable environment that empowers women to fully participate and excel in the digital world. It encompasses various elements such: Access to Resources, Education and Skill Development, Promoting STEM Fields, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Community Building and Support Networks, Leadership and Representation

VISSION

Empowering women with technology involves leveraging technological advancements to bridge gender gaps, promote equality, and provide opportunities for women in various aspects of life.

MOTTO

Innovation Without Boundaries: Empowering Women with Technology
